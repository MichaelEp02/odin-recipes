Shnitzel

Description

A shnizel is a thin slice of meat which is often breaded. The dish originated from Germany and is popular in many other countries.

Ingridients

  • Meat of your choice (preferably preferably chicken)
  • (optional) breadcrumbs and eggs
  • oil

Steps

  1. Take a sharp knife and cut your meat into thin slices
  2. (optional) Bread your meat with breadcrumbs by dipping it in eggs first and then in a plate of breadcrumbs on both sides
  3. Add oil to a pan and put it on medium/high heat
  4. Add your shnitzel to the pan and coot untill the meat is cooked through
  5. Serve and enjoy!
